Moving data between applications
Thinking about 3D GIS terrain data
  • The surface of the earth
    • Raster format
      Digital Elevation Models (DEM)
      Digital Surface Models (DSM)
    • Triangular Irregular Networks (TINs)
      Vector format
      Vertices (x,y,z) and triangles
      similar to mesh
    • Contours
Thinking about 3D GIS feature data
  • z-values
    • Vector format
    • polygons and extrusion
      for example: building footprints and heights
    • point clouds (x,y,z)
    • any kind of elevation information
      both above and below the earth's surface
Thinking about 3D Object data
  • Meshes
    • Like TINs, but can be closed
  • Models
    • Vertices
    • Faces
    • Materials and texture maps
    • Procedural surfaces
  • Almost never has geographical coordinates!!
Imports and Exports (3D design)
  • GIS to 3D design
    • Geographic coordinates are lost
      almost always
    • Layer information is lost
  • 3D Design to GIS
    • Materials and procedural textures are lost
      almost always
    • BIM information is lost
