The Symbolization Problem
How to understand geographic phenomena,
symbolize them,
and then re-present them on a map?

  • Select a measurement level (or find data for the level you are interested in)
    nominal, ordinal, interval, or ratio (categories to numbers, respectively)
  • Choose the dimension for the symbol
    point, line, or area
  • Repeat this process for all features that are desired on the map
