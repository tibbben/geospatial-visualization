Cartographic Process

  • identify problem and need
  • sketch out initial ideas
  • share, refine and reflect
  • make a draft or prototype
  • test, seek feedback, refine design
  • cartographer makes the map
  • users use the map

Cartographic Process

  • first decisions: medium, size, and format
  • process is iterative, adaptive
  • it is your process

Take Home

  • perfection is not possible, excellence is
  • cartographers often create garbage
  • make sure to END the process

Cartographic Process

Research:
census, survey, remote sensing
Cartography:
selection, classification, simplification, exaggeration, symbolization
Reading/Use:
analysis, interpretation
Each stage involves a fundamental transformation of information
